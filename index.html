About Us

H. Smiles in Miles Safaris is a passionate tour and travel company committed to turning every journey into a lasting memory. Based in Kenya, we specialize in car rentals, guided tours, and tailor-made travel experiences across East Africa. With a fleet of well-maintained vehicles and a team of professional drivers, we ensure comfort, safety, and reliability for every adventure. Clients can easily make prior bookings, and all our vehicles are regularly inspected and serviced for peace of mind. Whether you’re chasing the Big Five on a safari or exploring hidden gems off the beaten path, H. Smiles in Miles Safaris is your trusted companion on the road..

Our Services

  • Car Rentals: Choose from 4x4s, vans, and saloon cars — with or without a driver.
  • Chauffeur Services: Let our experienced guides take the wheel while you enjoy the view.
  • Safari Packages: Custom tours to top destinations in Kenya and beyond.
  • Corporate Rentals: Flexible long-term solutions for businesses and professionals.

How We Work

Clients make prior bookings through our online system or by contacting us directly. Each vehicle is thoroughly inspected and serviced before departure. We prioritize safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction on every trip.
